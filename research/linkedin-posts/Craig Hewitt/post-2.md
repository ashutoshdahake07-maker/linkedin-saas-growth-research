
Self sabotage is the reason most founders fail

And it's usually centered in over-engineering the whole process of marketing. 

Perfect being the enemy of Succes, they over-think, second-guess, and just generally procrastinate marketing at all.

Instead, put in 50 reps: 50 social posts, 50 videos, 50 cold emails...and then see what works, and what doesn't.

Guarantee that you'll learn more about what actually matters in those 50 reps than you would in months or "perfecting" your marketing strategy, which is really just a thin veil to say you're scared of failing.
