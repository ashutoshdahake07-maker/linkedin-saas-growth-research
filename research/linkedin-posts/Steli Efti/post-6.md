About half a year ago when I made the decision to focus the entire company on building the best in class voice agent in CRM, it wasn't as clear as now that things would work out this well.

We put the entire company on one bet. Every engineer. Every marketer. All of our resources. For over half a year.

We were going to build Chloe: a AI voice agent natively inside Close.

The obvious question: why would a CRM company building a voice agent? There are startups whose entire focus is AI voice. Why compete on their turf?

Here's the insight that drove everything:

A voice agent without CRM context isn’t useful to a small business.

When a standalone voice agent calls your lead, it's flying blind. It doesn't know your pipeline. It doesn’t know how to handle objections like you would. It doesn’t know your business inside and out.

Chloe is different because she lives inside Close. She has access to your lead list, your contact information, your email templates, your full customer history. She makes calls on your behalf and updates your CRM instantly and automatically.

That context is what makes the conversation actually good.

We had customers come to us during beta and tell us: "We spent four months building our own voice AI and integrating it with Close. We're throwing all of it away. Chloe is clearly better."

We could have shipped a voice agent in weeks. A lot of companies did. Quickly build something, put it on the website, announce how fast you moved, and get back to the next thing.

But I've watched too many products get sloppier and sloppier that way. The demo video is always 10x more impressive than the real thing.

I wanted the opposite. Watch our video, feel curious — then try the product and be genuinely surprised by how much better it actually is.

My bar for Chloe and the team was high. But I’m happy to say, they delivered. The bet paid off.

And here's what I'm most excited about: voice is just the beginning.

Calling inbound leads the moment they raise their hand — that's the highest-impact thing Chloe can do right now. But what we're really building is the full AI layer of the CRM. The teammate that lives inside your business, knows everything, and acts on it.

We're just getting started.
