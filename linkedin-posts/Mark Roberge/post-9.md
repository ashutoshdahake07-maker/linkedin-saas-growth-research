Grateful to be included in 2026: In Pursuit of AI Methodology alongside an impressive group of GTM operators. I was asked to share my 2026 predictions and here’s one that I have strong conviction in:

Sales enablement becomes the agentic operating layer of go-to-market.
For years, enablement meant content distribution and training programs.
That’s not where this is going.

In 2026, the biggest AI shift in GTM will sit inside the rep workflow:

AI running pre-meeting account research.

- Generating account-specific buyer agents for AE practice.
- Delivering real-time coaching during live meetings.
- Orchestrating follow-up and account planning.
- Continuously diagnosing rep skill gaps and prescribing next-best training.

This is all about workflow reinvention.

A few ripple effects I expect:

First, fewer traditional SDR roles.
If AI can handle real-time account research and personalize outreach based on ICP and persona data, the “research and prep” layer shrinks dramatically.

Second, buyers won’t tolerate generic messaging.
They’ll have AI filtering inbound outreach for relevance. If your message isn’t specific to their company, role, and use case, it won’t even reach them.

That raises the bar for quality.

Third, the messy tech stack starts to simplify. Instead of reps updating five systems and managers stitching together spreadsheets, AI creates a real-time source of truth.
Which gives frontline managers their time back to coach instead of inspect.
