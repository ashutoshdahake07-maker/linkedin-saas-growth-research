
The big shift in software (and in AI for that matter) in '26 will be the AI prompting you. 

you don't want to go visit the Stripe dashboard to look something up.

you damn sure don't want to visit GA4 or some other metrics account.

you want to work deeply on things that matter, and have a system that bubbles up important issues to you when (and only when) you need to know about them. 

We've conditioned users to have to go TO our product to use them, but the big change now is that Agents and AI systems will do all the work in the background (UI-less software), and will surface things to users proactively.

pretty sure i'm not wrong about this, but even if I am, i think thinking like this - the paradigm shift that we're experiencing right now - is what's going to keep us alive in the next 18 months.
