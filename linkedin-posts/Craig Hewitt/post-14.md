After 8 years of working on Castos all day, every day, we're embarking on a new product. 

Will be sharing what the product is tomorrow, but today I wanted to share a bit about how I chose the direction to go in...cause A LOT has changed about how/why/where to build products in the past few years. 

Firstly, what it CAN'T be: 

- can't be a basic CRUD app - those will be free or get demolished by AI
- can't be Enterprise - I just don't have any desire to sit through eternal sales processes. for better or worse I like the B2Prosumer space
- can't be too cheap - this is a tough one and honestly where we ended up kinda missing. 
- can't take more than a month to build. I built this entire product in about a month with Claude Code
- can't be mission critical. We're not building the next Stripe here. Just too much pressure to be that close to customers money

Now, what it NEEDS to be: 

- 'adjacent' to our existing customer base and audience at Castos. Doesn't have to be directly in the podcasting space, but something that our customers/audience at Castos can relate to. I think Basecamp has done a great job at this.
- heavily AI enhanced. Building non-AI SaaS these days is silly, and i'd never do it
- really sticky: one of the best traits of Castos from a business model perspective is that we have extremely low churn...makes everything about building the business easier. We've built a lot of this into the new product.
- in a growing market. This is probably the most important. Rising tides lifts all boats. We nailed this one.
- Has network effect. Did pretty good on this one, but not a 10/10. This is what opens up FREE tiers
- Has a generous Free tier - this is something we intentionally don't have with Castos, and I think can be a big driver of growth. Free users you can market to FOREVER

I had one false start on the path to this new product, and the biggest lesson from it was this: 

+ Love the space you're building in +

I nailed this with Castos 8 years ago...podcasting is such a fun market. And the new product is in a market I'm even more passionate about. 

On paper everything about this new product lines up so well with what we've already built with Castos, and I can't wait to share it with y'all.
