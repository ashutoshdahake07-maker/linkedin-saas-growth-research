Ads coming to ChatGPT!!!

This is going to be the biggest marketing opportunity of the decade, if you do it right. 

Here's what you need to know: 

→ Ads will appear for free and Go tier users only 
→ Plus, Pro, and Enterprise users will stay ad-free 
→ Ads will appear at the bottom of responses
→ Ads do not influence the answers ChatGPT gives you 
→ Ads will be clearly labeled
→ No user data sold to advertisers (for now)
→ No ads on sensitive topics (health, politics, mental health) 

Early on in every new marketing channel, there is a huge arbitrage opportunity. 

Remember FB ads from 2014?! Many people got very rich then.
