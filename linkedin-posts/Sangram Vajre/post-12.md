i've built 3 communities in the last 10 years.

here's what i learned about pricing and value:

community #1: FlipMyFunnel (free)
→ 100,000 people
→ broad audience
→ lots of lurkers, low commitment

goal: build the ABM category

community #2: Peak Community ($10/month)
→ 1,000+ marketers aspiring to become CMOs
→ multiple events per week to keep it running
→ good engagement, but constant effort to maintain
→ made me 

goal: make more B2B marketers CMOs.

community #3: GTM Partners ($10K+/year)
→ 100+ certified partners (fractional CMOs, CROs, Ops leaders)
→ partners building $500K+/year businesses
→ routinely hear: "this has changed my life"

goal: make more GTM fractionals financially free.

same me. completely different outcomes.

the pattern is clear:

the more niche you go, the more you can charge.
the more you charge, the more value you can provide.
the more value you provide, the higher the quality of outcomes. 

and here's what nobody tells you about premium communities:

it's not the AI tools or the lead gen that changes lives.

it's the quality of people you surround yourself with.

when you're in a room with people who are building $500K+ businesses, you start thinking bigger. you stop making excuses. you reach higher.

free communities attract everyone.
premium communities attract the committed.

which room are you in?
