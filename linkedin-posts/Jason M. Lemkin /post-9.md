So a few months ago we got a rough email from a PR agency for a top AI startup. They said never reach out to that hot AI startup ever again.

They said their executive had a terrible experience with SaaStr AI and no one there wanted to work with us again.

That was tough to hear. I think we really treat folks well and are super grateful to all our speakers.

But you have to take the criticism.

Then, surprise, the other day ... that executive reached out directly to us!

We asked what the issue was, and he said he had no idea, he had a great experience with SaaStr AI.

And ... they had fired that PR firm.

Just a reminder to monitor all your agents carefully. Both AI. And human.

They are speaking for you. Make sure you know what they are saying.
