
What’s the latest “black box” sales hack that gets cold prospects to ACTUALLY listen? 

 🗣️ Voice memos.

Picture this: You’re scrolling LinkedIn. Another cold pitch slides into your inbox.

Text? Ignore.
Wall of paragraphs? Delete.

But a one-minute voice memo—no context, no subject line—just sitting there like a mysterious black box, daring you to press play? 

Now that’s different. You have to press play.

Here’s why voice memos in cold outreach are a cheat code right now:

👀 Curiosity takes over – It’s harder NOT to press play than to just listen.

✅ Low friction – One minute feels easy. A five-paragraph message? Not so much.

❤️ Feels personal – Voice memos are an intimate medium usually meant for your inner circle—family, close friends. They feel direct and personalized, not mass-blasted.

Voice memos haven’t been overused (yet) as a cold outreach hack. In a world of inbox spam, voice memos cut through the noise.

And sure, getting your prospect to listen to the message doesn’t guarantee a response. But it means you just won the hardest battle: getting their attention.

📩 Where to send your voice memos: LinkedIn DMs, WhatsApp, Instagram, X—anywhere that allows it. (We don’t recommend using email. Too much spam risk.)
