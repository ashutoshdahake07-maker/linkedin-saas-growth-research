A feature you may not have heard of is OpenAI's Sites. It enables you to post a website from a basic prompt that's hosted on their servers.

Most people aren't trying to build a scalable, monetizable SaaS but need mini-apps to help them or their teammates (the semi-pros are hosting their apps on Vercel or Railway).

This is interesting because it's the first clear move that threatens the consumer-facing vibe coding apps (Bolt, v0, Replit, Loveable etc').

I don't expect Anthropic to follow (it generally lags behind on consumer-facing features, e.g. image generation, video, voice is more advanced, canvas feature). But then again, I didn't expect them to sponsor the Super Bowl either as assumed they'd double down on the B2B route.
