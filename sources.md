## Selection Methodology

I chose these 10 because:

1. **They've actually built something** — Not commentators. Practitioners.
2. **They post regularly** — Not dormant accounts or one-hit wonders.
3. **Their audience is engaged** — High follower count means nothing if engagement is dead.
4. **They're diverse** — Different niches (sales, product, metrics, positioning, content, etc.) so I can see patterns across different domains.
5. **They're not obvious** — Not the first 10 results on Google. I had to think about who's actually moved the needle.
